Important safety information

A daily medication that helps restore testosterone to healthy levels, designed to fit into your morning routine.

Take daily with a high-fat meal for optimal absorption, ideally with breakfast. Stay consistent for best results.

Oral TRT is generally well tolerated. The most commonly reported side effects include GI symptoms (nausea, upset stomach), headache, and increases in red blood cell count and blood pressure — both of which your provider will monitor with regular lab work. As with all testosterone therapies, rare but serious side effects are possible. Oral TRT is not recommended for men with uncontrolled high blood pressure. Your provider will review your complete health profile before prescribing and monitor your labs throughout treatment.
