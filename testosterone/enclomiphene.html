Important safety information

Daily medication that signals your body to increase its own testosterone production to help you restore healthy levels without shutting down what's already working. Enclomiphene can work well for men with secondary hypogonadism, but it is not effective for men who have a diagnosis of primary hypogonadism.

One oral dose each day. Simple routine, taken with or without food.

Most men experience minimal side effects. When they occur, they're usually temporary and mild. Common side effects include: GI upset, headache, and increased estrogen levels. Seek medical attention immediately if there is an allergic reaction or if side effects are severe.
