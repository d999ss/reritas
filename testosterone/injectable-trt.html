Important safety information

Testosterone cypionate that helps replace what your body's no longer producing, so you may start to reclaim the energy, drive, and performance you remember.

Your doctor determines your dosing schedule, typically 1-2 times per week. Most men find the routine straightforward once they get started.

The most commonly reported side effects with injectable TRT are injection site reactions (redness, bruising), increases in red blood cell count, elevated blood pressure, and headache — all of which your provider will monitor with regular labs. Some men notice mood fluctuations in the days before their next injection as levels dip; more frequent dosing often reduces this. As with all testosterone therapies, serious side effects including blood clots and cardiovascular events are possible. Injectable TRT is not recommended for men with uncontrolled hypertension or certain cardiovascular conditions. Your provider will review your complete health profile before prescribing. Seek medical help immediately if you experience severe side effects or a reaction to the medication.
