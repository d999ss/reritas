Important safety information

Bioidentical testosterone cream is applied daily to your skin. An easy, needle-free way to restore healthy levels and take back what low T has taken.

Apply once daily to clean, dry skin. Let it dry completely before getting dressed. Your doctor determines the right amount based on your labs.

Common side effects include skin irritation at the application site, acne, and changes in red blood cell levels. Serious side effects are possible. Seek medical attention immediately if you experience severe side effects. Important: Testosterone gel must never come into contact with women, children, or pets — accidental transfer can cause serious hormonal effects. Your provider will review your complete risk profile and monitor your labs throughout treatment.

Testosterone gel can transfer to others through direct skin contact. To prevent transfer: allow the gel to dry completely before dressing, wash the application site with soap and water before any skin-to-skin contact, and keep the area covered around women and children. Pregnant women in particular should avoid any contact, as testosterone exposure poses risks to fetal development. If accidental contact occurs, the exposed person should wash the area with soap and water immediately.

Many men achieve excellent results with topical TRT. The right choice depends on your labs, how your body absorbs the medication, and your personal preference.
