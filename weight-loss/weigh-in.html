Three medications, one personalized plan

Bupropion

Helps reduce food cravings by affecting neurotransmitters in your brain, so eating less can feel more natural instead of forced.

Naltrexone

Works with bupropion to help suppress appetite by influencing feedback signals in your brain that can counteract weight‑loss efforts.

Metformin

Helps regulate blood sugar levels and support feelings of fullness, supporting healthy metabolism and may help reduce hunger spikes.
