Erectile dysfunction is extremely common. In fact, it affects more than 30 million men in the USA alone.

That’s just the number of people who report their symptoms to their doctors. The age range is vast.

Even men in their early 20s are experiencing symptoms of E.D. and getting diagnosed due to contributing factors such as depression/anxiety, psychiatric medications, COVID infection, and obesity to name a few.

Let’s take a critical look at available treatment options, and find the best one for you.

Traditional Pill Treatments

Sildenafil, in the form of a pill, takes 30-60 minutes to start working because it needs to be processed by your digestive system and liver.

The lengthy or inconsistent onset time is a common complaint from those who use the pill form.

Onset time is also highly contingent upon how much or how little you’ve had to eat.

For example, if you've had a hearty meal, chances are you’ll be waiting closer to an hour or more to see results.

This leaves timely sexual success to chance, which isn’t a great feeling. Tadalafil, in pill form, is known as “the weekend pill” for its long-lasting effects.

The effects are not as fast to onset, and wear off after 36 hours.

There are those who opt for a 5mg daily dose of tadalafil so that the meds are constantly in their system.

However, this means having to remember to take a pill daily. With both of these traditional options, side effects (headaches, soreness, flushing, etc) can be unmitigated because they typically come in standard doses.

Chewable Tablets

Chewable tablets aren’t much better than their pill counterparts. Their function is to make ingesting the medicine easier than swallowing a pill.

However, just like pills, the medication in a chewable tablet must go through the liver and digestive system before entering the bloodstream.

That means they still take about 30-60 minutes to start working. Food intake is also a factor to consider with chewable tablets.

Another downside to chewable tablets is the main chewable brands are infamously bitter.

The chalk texture coats your mouth and gets stuck in your teeth.

If that’s not bad enough, add the bitterness of sildenafil or tadalafil, and of course, the embarrassing blue tongue.
