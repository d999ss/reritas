Who Is Rugiet Health?

March 12, 2026

About Rugiet Health: Personalized Medicine, Built Different

Rugiet Health exists because the standard of care for men's sexual health wasn't good enough — and four people decided to do something about it.

We're the company behind Rugiet Ready, a compounded ED treatment prescribed by board-certified physicians through our telehealth platform. But we're more than an ED brand. We're a new model for how medicine should work: personalized, accessible, stigma-free, and built around the individual — not a one-size-fits-all formula invented decades ago.

What Makes Rugiet Health Different

Most pharmaceutical companies sell you the same drug that's been on the market since 1998, repackaged in modern branding at a higher price. We don't do that.

Rugiet Health specializes in compounded pharmaceuticals — customized formulations that combine multiple active ingredients into a single, optimized treatment. Rather than choosing between ingredient A or ingredient B, our patients get a personalized blend calibrated to their specific needs, body, and health goals.

Our compounding pharmacies are fully USA-licensed and operate in strict compliance with state and federal law. Because cutting corners in healthcare isn't something we're willing to do — not for convenience, not for cost, not for anyone.

The Problem We Set Out to Solve

Cast your mind back to 1998. Viagra hits the market and changes the conversation around erectile dysfunction overnight. In 2003, Cialis follows — "the weekend pill," effective for up to 36 hours, another genuine breakthrough for its time.

Here's the thing: that was over two decades ago.

Medical science has advanced enormously since then. But the ED treatment market? Not so much. The same generic versions of sildenafil and tadalafil still dominate — in pill form, with the same slow and unpredictable onset (up to 60 minutes or more), the same limitations, and the same one-size-fits-all approach. Just dressed up in sleeker packaging.

Meanwhile, the telehealth companies that emerged to distribute these medications built their brands on shame. Awkward doctor visits. Hushed tones. Marketing that treated ED like a dirty secret — despite the fact that it affects the majority of men at some point in their lives.

We looked at all of this and thought: there has to be a better way.

How Rugiet Health Was Born

Rugiet Health was founded by four college friends who went on to build careers in pharmacy, medicine, engineering, and investment. They came together around a shared conviction: that patients deserved more than what the market was offering.

The breakthrough came when co-founder and pharmacist Dr. Ken Bains developed a sublingual compound combining sildenafil and tadalafil — a formulation that dissolved under the tongue, absorbed directly into the bloodstream, and worked in a fraction of the time traditional pills required. When the group saw what it could do, they knew they had something worth building around.

So they built it properly.

They developed a secure, advanced digital health platform. They contracted a network of board-certified physicians to prescribe through it. They designed a patient experience that treated men with dignity rather than embarrassment. And they refused to launch until every piece of it — the science, the technology, the care model — met the standard they'd set for themselves.

As Dr. Vipul Khanpara, MD, Chief Medical Officer and Co-Founder, puts it:

"I genuinely feel that Rugiet Health has developed a revolutionary treatment for ED in Rugiet Ready. Being able to help men overcome their challenges with ED and develop stronger relationships with their partners — while striving to remove the stigma that has become associated with ED — is something I have become very passionate about."

The Rugiet Health Platform

The Rugiet Health Digital Health Clinic is built to make personalized care genuinely accessible — without sacrificing quality at any step.

Our platform connects patients, physicians, and pharmacies in a seamless, secure experience that includes a thorough medical intake questionnaire, fast access to board-certified doctors, advanced digital security, real human customer support (no bots), flexible trial and membership options, and a straightforward system for tracking and managing your treatment.

Every prescription is reviewed by a licensed physician. Every formulation is compounded by a USA-licensed pharmacy. And every patient gets ongoing access to providers who can adjust their treatment as needed — because personalized medicine doesn't stop at the first prescription.

What's Next for Rugiet Health

Rugiet Ready is where we started. It's not where we're stopping.

Our telehealth infrastructure was built to scale — and to bring the same personalized, compounded approach to a growing range of conditions beyond ED. Because the problem we identified in men's sexual health isn't unique to that category. Across medicine, patients are being handed generic, mass-market solutions when what they actually need is something built for them.

That's the gap Rugiet Health was built to close.

We do medicine right. And we're just getting started.
